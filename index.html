About us Kuraz Consulting is a training and consulting company that was established in October, 2020, by a team of social scientists, business and development studies professionals, IT experts, and technical engineers, focusing on promoting investment, engineering, and economic development in Ethiopia. The Kuraz Consulting company was established to address and solve economic complexity, investment, technical and social issues in the Somali regional state and the wider region of Ethiopia through scientific study, policy papers, training and consultancy services with the collaboration of public development agencies and private sectors. We provide high-quality technical support services for financial companies, service businesses, textile, leather, garment, waste recycling companies, and various manufacturing organizations working across a broad range of development sectors. We specialize in conducting large-scale surveys and studies and designing projects, monitoring and evaluation systems. Our rigorous research and surveys are tailored and responsive to the research needs at hand. We help our clients maximize the accuracy and reliability of the information supporting their programs by carrying out qualitative and quantitative research, designing monitoring and public economic systems, and providing extensive capacity-building support. Our work brings together an unparalleled combination of local and international expertise, in addition to a wide breadth of multi-country experience. This diversity enables us to provide nuanced and appropriate services that assist social and economic development, particularly in agriculture, industrial development, livelihoods, food security, education, gender, health and nutrition, the environment, institutional development, and government systems strengthening.
